How to Play PixelRealms

🌱 Expand

You earn 1 expansion token per hour (max 24 stacked) — shown as 🎯 in the top bar. Switch to Expand mode and click an unclaimed tile next to your territory to claim it for 1 token. Water tiles can't be claimed.

⚔️ Attack

Switch to Attack mode and click an enemy tile next to your territory. Each attack costs gold. Your army size vs. their army size (modified by terrain defense — hills and mountains defend better) decides the outcome. Winning captures the tile and damages both armies; losing still costs you troops. Eliminate a nation by taking all their pixels and reducing their army to 1.

🏗️ Build

Switch to Build mode and click one of your own tiles, then open the Build tab on the right and choose a structure:

  • 🌾 Farm — increases income per pixel
  • ⚔️ Barracks — instantly adds army units
  • 🏪 Market — boosts trade income
  • 🧱 Wall — boosts defense on that tile
  • ⛏️ Mine — instant gold + flat income boost

Only one building per tile. Buildings appear on the map for every player to see.

💰 Economy

Every pixel generates gold per hour based on your income rate. Your army costs gold upkeep per pixel. Watch the Net income in My Realm — if it's negative, build farms/mines or shrink your army's upkeep burden.

🤝 Trade

Send gold to other nations from the Trade tab — useful for alliances or buying peace.

🗺️ Map

Each nation's territory is outlined in grey with its name shown over its land. Scroll to zoom, drag to pan, and click a nation in the Nations tab to jump to it.
