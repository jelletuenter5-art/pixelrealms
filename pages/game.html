How to Play PixelRealms

🗺️ The Map

PixelRealms is a real-time multiplayer strategy game. Every player controls a nation on a shared pixel map. Scroll to zoom, drag to pan. Tap the 🗺️ terrain button (bottom-left) to reveal the landscape — grass, hills, mountains, desert, and water all affect income and combat. Click any nation in the Nations tab to jump to it on the map.

🌱 Expanding Your Territory

You earn 1 expansion token per hour (max 24 stacked), shown as 🎯 in the top bar. Switch to Expand mode and click any unclaimed tile directly adjacent to your territory to claim it for 1 token. Water tiles cannot be claimed on foot — use a boat for that. You can also buy tokens for 1,000 💰 each when you need to expand faster.

🏗️ Buildings

Switch to Build mode and click one of your tiles to open the building menu. Clicking a tile that already has a building opens a manage dialog where you can upgrade (farms only), or sell the building for a 25% gold refund.

⚠️ Building cap: 1 building per 2 pixels. A 10-pixel nation can have at most 5 buildings. Expand to unlock more slots.

💰 Economy

Each pixel earns 1.5 💰/hr base income. Buildings add on top of this. Territory upkeep scales with your size — small nations pay far less:

≤15 pixels → 0.05 💰/px/hr upkeep
16–40 pixels → 0.15 💰/px/hr upkeep
41–80 pixels → 0.22 💰/px/hr upkeep
81+ pixels → 0.30 💰/px/hr upkeep

Border upkeep also scales — tiles of yours touching an enemy add upkeep per border pixel:

≤15 pixels → 2 💰/hr per border pixel
16–40 pixels → 5 💰/hr per border pixel
41–80 pixels → 8 💰/hr per border pixel
81+ pixels → 10 💰/hr per border pixel

Other costs: 0.5 💰/hr per army unit. Markets reduce territory upkeep further. Check My Realm → Balance to see your live net gold/hr. Gold never drops below 0.

👥 Population & 🌽 Food

Each pixel gives 15 population, which unlocks barracks (100 pop each). Your land produces 0.5 food/hr per pixel and consumes 0.2 food/hr per pixel. Your army eats 0.1 food/hr per unit at peace — this rises sharply during war. Build farms to keep your food in surplus. Farms produce 5 food/hr each and can be upgraded for 200g to produce 15 food/hr. If your stored food drops below 50% of your population, your gold income is penalised by up to −80% — always stockpile food before going to war.

⚓ Harbours & Boats

Build a Harbour (⚓ 180g) on any land tile bordering water. Then switch to Boat mode and click any reachable land tile across water — the game auto-detects whether to expand or attack. The boat departs from your nearest harbour and travels at 4 minutes per tile.

Expand by boat: costs 1 expansion token. If the tile is taken before arrival, your token is refunded.
Attack by boat: combat resolves on arrival using current army sizes.
All boats are visible to everyone. You can cancel your own boat at any time from the bottom panel.

⚔️ Attack & War

Switch to Attack mode and click an enemy tile bordering your territory. Your army vs. their army — modified by terrain (hills and mountains defend better) — decides the outcome. Both sides lose troops win or lose. Capture all of an enemy's pixels to eliminate them. If a captured tile has a building, there's a 50% chance it transfers to you and a 50% chance it's destroyed.

Each attack raises your war aggression, which increases your army's food consumption. Aggression decays over time (faster with markets). Sustained wars without enough food will cripple your economy — plan ahead.

💬 Chat

Open the Chat tab to talk with other players in real time. Messages show your nation name and colour. If a message breaks the rules, tap 🚩 to report it to the admin.

🤝 Diplomacy

Open the Diplomacy tab to send gold to another nation or propose a ceasefire. Set the duration (in hours) and an optional gold offer — the other nation must accept before it takes effect. While a ceasefire is active, neither side can attack the other. Ceasefires appear in the Events tab when agreed.
