How to Play PixelRealms

🌱 Expand

You earn 1 expansion token per hour (max 24 stacked) — shown as 🎯 in the top bar. Switch to Expand mode and click an unclaimed tile next to your territory to claim it for 1 token. Water tiles can't be claimed. You can also buy tokens for 1000 gold each.

⚔️ Attack

Switch to Attack mode and click an enemy tile next to your territory. Your army size vs. their army size (modified by terrain defense — hills and mountains defend better) decides the outcome. Winning captures the tile and damages both armies; losing still costs you troops. Eliminate a nation by taking all their pixels.

🏗️ Build

Switch to Build mode and click one of your own tiles to build or manage a building there. Clicking a tile with an existing building shows a popup where you can delete it permanently.

  • 🌾 Farm (80g) — +0.2 gold/pixel/hr nation-wide. Best once you have many pixels.
  • ⛏️ Mine (80g) — +8 gold/hr flat plus a one-time +0.1 gold/pixel/hr bonus (regardless of how many mines you own). Build one early for the pixel bonus, then more for flat income.
  • 🏪 Market (100g) — reduces territory upkeep by 0.03/pixel/hr nation-wide. Stack them to lower costs, but upkeep can't go below 0.
  • ⚔️ Barracks (120g) — instantly adds 20 army strength.
  • 🧱 Wall (60g) — +50% defense on that specific tile only. Place on key frontline tiles.
💰 Economy

Each pixel earns 1 gold/hr base. Farms raise this for your whole nation. Your army costs 0.25 gold/hr per unit, and territory costs 0.3 gold/pixel/hr (markets reduce this). Watch Net gold/hr in My Realm — if it's negative your gold drains. Build mines early, farms when large, markets to offset a big army.

🤝 Trade

Send gold to other nations from the Trade tab — useful for alliances or buying peace.

🗺️ Map

Each nation's territory is outlined in grey with its name shown over its land. Scroll to zoom, drag to pan, and click a nation in the Nations tab to jump to it.
